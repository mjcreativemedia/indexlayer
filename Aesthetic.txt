## IndexLayer Aesthetic

Minimal.
Technical.
Readable.
Calm.

The design philosophy is:

```text id="9jlwm9"
clarity over decoration
```

We want the site to feel:

* stable
* modern
* intelligent
* trustworthy
* highly legible

Not like a marketing agency.

More like:

```text id="q6o29h"
a modern publishing infrastructure lab
```

---

# Visual Direction

## Typography First

Large fonts.
Generous spacing.
Strong hierarchy.

Reading should feel effortless.

We want:

* oversized headlines
* readable paragraph widths
* high line-height
* clean sans-serif typography
* minimal visual noise

Very content-focused.

---

# Minimal UI

No heavy gradients.
No flashy animations.
No clutter.

Just:

* whitespace
* typography
* subtle dividers
* structured layouts
* clean cards
* soft green accents

The interface should disappear behind the content.

---

# OpenAI-Inspired Simplicity

The inspiration is modern AI product design:

* simple layouts
* quiet confidence
* restrained colors
* minimal chrome
* clean spacing
* highly readable content blocks

The site should feel:

```text id="0v4vxb"
engineered
not decorated
```

---

# Color System

Primary palette:

* white backgrounds
* soft gray structure
* muted green accents
* dark neutral text

Very light theme.

Green should feel:

* calm
* technical
* infrastructure-oriented

Not neon.
Not startup-hype green.

---

# Motion Philosophy

Minimal motion.

Only subtle:

* fades
* hover transitions
* small layout shifts

No excessive:

* parallax
* complex animations
* heavy interactions

The site should feel fast and stable.

---

# Content Presentation

The content itself is the design.

We want:

* diagrams
* architecture notes
* audit screenshots
* GSC screenshots
* code snippets
* publishing breakdowns
* case studies

Presented very cleanly.

Almost like:

```text id="k0zxt0"
technical research notes
```

---

# Overall Feeling

The aesthetic should communicate:

```text id="u7gdc4"
we understand the internet deeply
```

without needing to say it directly.

Simple.
Precise.
Highly readable.
Technically calm.
Modern.
Unbreakable.
